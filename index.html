Frequently Asked
Questions

Why is submission paid? ▾
A small fee keeps quality high and filters spam. Every paid idea gets genuine consideration. Voting is always 100% free.
Can I vote without an account? ▾
Yes! Voting is always free and requires no account. Just click the upvote arrow on any idea card.
How does account creation work? ▾
When you submit and pay, an account is automatically created using your email. Check your inbox for a login link. You can also sign in with Google.
How are winners chosen? ▾
Every week I pick the top-voted ideas and film them. Ideas tagged "Planned" are already scheduled. "Filmed" means the video is live on YouTube!
What is a Featured Submission? ▾
For $14.99, your idea is pinned to the very top of the board for 48 hours — giving it massive visibility and a head start on votes.
Will I be credited in the video? ▾
Yes — your username is mentioned in the video description, and often in the video itself!
Can I edit my idea after submitting? ▾
Yes! Log into your dashboard and you can edit the title and description of any Open or Planned idea.
